Reflection and Learnings about the course.

Because of all the challenges that I and my classmates had to face throughout this course, I'll be honest and say that I never imagined I would finish Managing Enterprise. This final product is a declaration that my efforts have been appreciated. I would like to add that all of the information I learned from this course played a significant role in forming the person I am today. I will continue learning about new automations, network capabilities, and all the services if resources permit. I would create my own automatic network if I had the time and resources to do it. Critical thinking is the most useful skill I've learned from this training.

My experience

After taking online classes for the previous two years, this course marked my first in-person class experience. I had anticipated that readjusting to the entire situation would be difficult, but thanks to a warmly welcome professor and approachable, nice classmates, it was simple. I was aware that I could ask my classmates or lecturer for aid if I needed it with a task and they would do it. The exercises in this course were simple to comprehend but challenging to complete, which forced me to apply my critical thinking skills. Overall, it was an enjoyable experience with both challenges and learning opportunities.
